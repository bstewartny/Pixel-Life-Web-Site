Pixel Life

Version 1.0

Pixel Life for iPhone and iPad is a social photo browsing app. Comment, like and view photos from your Facebook friends on your iPhone and iPad.

  • Universal app runs on both iPhone and iPad with a single purchase
  • Supports multiple Facebook accounts for sharing between family members, etc.
  • Easily browse through all of your Facebook friends photo albums
  • Add comments to photo albums and individual photos
  • 'Like' photo albums and individual photos on Facebook
  • View a photo slideshow using your iPad as a digital photo frame
Available on the iPhone

Requirements: Compatible with iPhone, iPad and iPod touch. Requires iPhone OS 4.2 or later. WiFi, Edge, or 3G network connection required.

Why Facebook photos?

We wanted a simple app that performed well for browsing all of our Facebook friends photo albums, and was focused on photo viewing.

How much does it cost?

The initial release is priced at $1.99 for a universal app that runs on both iPhone and iPad. You only need to purchase the app once to run it on both your iPhone and iPad.

What features will you add?

We plan to add support for other social network photos, such as Flickr and Instagram, as well as improving the photo viewing experience.
